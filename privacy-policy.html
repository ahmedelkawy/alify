Privacy Policy for Alify

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

Welcome to Alify ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect and Access

To provide you with the best educational and interactive experience, Alify accesses or utilizes certain features on your device:

  • Camera Access: With your explicit permission, the app accesses your device's camera to power the AI Smart Scanner feature. This allows you to instantly identify animal breeds. Images captured by the camera are processed securely in real-time and are never saved, stored, or shared on any external servers.
  • Local Storage (SQLite & SharedPreferences): The app utilizes local device storage to manage your points balance, favorites list, language preferences, and scheduled reminders. This data remains entirely inside your device and is under your direct control.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising

Our application integrates trusted third-party SDKs to optimize performance, analyze trends, and display advertisements that fund our free content:

  • Google AdMob: We show advertisements (such as Rewarded Video Ads) within the app. AdMob may collect and use anonymous data, device identifiers, or cookies to display personalized or non-personalized ads relevant to you.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: We collect anonymized usage statistics and diagnostic events (such as screen views, event counts, ad impressions, and system interactions) to monitor the app's stability and improve future updates.

3. Notifications and Alerts

Alify provides smart fishing alerts, bait reminders, and periodic pet care notifications. These alerts are managed locally via our system architecture. You retain complete control over these notices and can easily modify or disable them through the app's alerts tab or your mobile OS settings.

4. Children's Privacy

Alify is an educational encyclopedia designed for users of all ages, including families. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

5. Data Security

Because Alify operates without forcing users to create accounts or central database profiles, your data is inherently protected by your phone's security systems. We implement standard software safety boundaries to protect your local configurations.
